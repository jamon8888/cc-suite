# Roadmap Example: V1 Launch of "Solo CRM"

## NOW (Building the Core)

- **Theme**: Zero-Admin Invoicing.
- **Problem**: Users hate manual data entry for invoices.
- **Outcome**: Time to invoice < 60 seconds.
- **Solution**: `/solo:invoice` AI command.

## NEXT (Scaling Value)

- **Theme**: Smart Pipeline Intelligence.
- **Problem**: Users forget to follow up with leads.
- **Outcome**: 50% decrease in lead "Staleness."
- **Solution**: Automation hooks for Slack/Email reminders.

## LATER (Future Vision)

- **Theme**: Predictive Revenue forecasting.
- **Problem**: Users don't know if they'll hit their Q4 income targets.
- **Outcome**: +/- 10% forecast accuracy.
- **Solution**: Financial health analytics suite.
