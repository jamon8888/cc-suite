## What to provide
- **Context**: What screen, flow, or feature?
- **User state**: What is the user trying to do? How are they feeling?
- **Tone**: Formal, friendly, empathetic, playful?
- **Constraints**: Character limits, platform guidelines?

## Output includes
- Recommended copy with rationale
- 2–3 alternatives with tone and use-case notes
- Localization notes (idioms to avoid, expansion considerations)
