# /research — Strategic Research

Deploy two independent research agents to investigate a strategic topic, with a validator to cross-check findings.

## Usage

```
/research <topic to research>
```

### Arguments

- `topic to research` — The market, industry, competitive landscape, or strategic question to investigate

## Workflow

Invoke the **research** skill:
1. **researcher-a**: Direct angle (market data, financials, benchmarks)
2. **researcher-b**: Complementary angle (competitive dynamics, case studies, contrarian evidence)
3. **validator**: Cross-checks both for consistency, source quality, and gaps

## Output

A validated research package with:
- Executive summary of findings
- Source-registry with credibility scores (CS-1 to CS-4)
- Identified gaps and under-explored areas
