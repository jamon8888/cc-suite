## Step 3 — Self-serving bias check

Rate the following on a scale of 1 (strongly disagree) to 5 (strongly agree):

| Statement | Score |
|---|---|
| "Successes in this project were primarily due to our decisions and skill" | |
| "Failures in this project were primarily due to external factors" | |

**Diagnosis**: If both scores are ≥ 4, Self-Serving Bias (ID 28) is likely active.
Apply 20% correction: attribute more of the success to external factors, more of the failure to internal decisions.

## Step 4 — Transferable learning statement

Complete this sentence:
> "The [specific cognitive failure or process gap] caused us to [specific decision error].
> Next time, we will [specific structural change] so that [observable outcome that would show it worked]."

**Example**:
> "Our survivorship-biased benchmarks caused us to overestimate market growth. Next time,
> we will explicitly ask 'what companies tried this and failed?' before using growth benchmarks,
> so our reference class includes both survivors and failures."
