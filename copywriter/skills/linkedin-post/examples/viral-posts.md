# Viral Post Examples (The Swipe File)

Study the structure (not the content) of these high-performing posts.

## Example 1: The "Systems" Post

**Structure:** Hook -> Problem -> Solution List -> CTA.

> **(Hook)** You don't need more motivation. You need a better system.
>
> **(Agitation)** Motivation is fleeting. It relies on feelings. And feelings lie.
>
> **(Pivot)** Systems are reliable. They rely on logic.
>
> **(The Meat)** Here is the 3-part system I use to write every day:
>
> 1. The Trigger: Same time, same place.
> 2. The Constraint: 30 minutes only.
> 3. The Reward: No coffee until word count is hit.
>
> **(CTA)** What's your daily writing ritual?

## Example 2: The "Hard Truth" Post

**Structure:** Hook -> Story -> Lesson -> CTA.

> **(Hook)** I fired my biggest client today.
>
> **(Context)** They paid me $5k/mo. They were a recognizable brand.
>
> **(Conflict)** But they treated my team like servants. They skipped payments. They demanded 24/7 access.
>
> **(Resolution)** So, I sent the email. "We are effectively terminating..."
>
> **(The Lesson)** Money is renewable. Sanity is not. Never trade your peace for a paycheck.
>
> **(CTA)** Have you ever fired a client?

## Example 3: The "Curated List" Post

**Structure:** Hook -> List -> Summary.

> **(Hook)** ChatGPT is cool. But these 5 AI tools are specifically for Solopreneurs:
>
> **(The List)**
>
> 1. **Jasper**: For long-form writing.
> 2. **Perplexity**: For deep research.
> 3. **MidJourney**: For unique visuals.
> 4. **Otter.ai**: For meeting notes.
> 5. **Gamma**: For slide decks.
>
> **(Summary)** Don't use AI to replace you. Use it to duplicate you.
