# Blog Structure Template (The Rank-Worthy Framework)

Use this skeleton to structure every blog post. It is optimized for Google's "Helpful Content" update and featured snippets.

## 1. The Hook (Introduction)

_Goal: Match Intent + Agitate Problem._

- **The Problem Statement**: "If you've ever tried to [Action], you know how hard it is."
- **The Agitation**: "Most advice tells you to [Bad Advice], which leads to [Bad Result]."
- **The Solution (Thesis)**: "But there is a better way: [The Mechanism]."
- **The Promise**: "In this guide, you will learn [Benefit 1], [Benefit 2], and [Benefit 3]."

---

## 2. The Definition (Snippet Bait)

_Goal: Win the 'What is X?' Snippet._

## What is [Keyword]?

[Keyword] is the process of [Definition]. Unlike [Alternative], it focuses on [Unique Quality].

---

## 3. The Core Steps (The Meat)

_Goal: Comprehensive Coverage._

## Step 1: [Actionable Step Name]

- **The Why**: Explain the logic.
- **The How**: Bullet points of specific actions.
- **The Example**: "For example, if you are a [Avatar], you would do X."
  > [!TIP]
  > [Specific actionable tip related to this step]

## Step 2: [Actionable Step Name]

...

---

## 4. The Expert Nuance (E-E-A-T Injection)

_Goal: Demonstrate Experience._

## Common Mistakes (And How to Fix Them)

- **Mistake 1**: [Description].
- **Fix**: [Correction].
- **My Experience**: "I used to do this, until I realized..."

---

## 5. The Conclusion

_Goal: Conversion._

## Final Thoughts

Summarize the main takeaway in 2 sentences.

**Ready to start?**
[Call to Action linking to Offer].
