## Journalist Research Best Practices

1. **Always read the journalist's last 10 articles** before pitching — pitch to their demonstrated interests, not their beat title
2. **LinkedIn is more reliable than email** for first contact with French journalists — many check it daily
3. **Twitter/X for breaking news journalists** — many are most responsive on X for news-reactive pitches
4. **Never pitch the same story to competing publications simultaneously** — exclusivity wins trust
5. **Morning pitch window**: 8–10 AM is optimal for news journalists. Features journalists: any time except Friday afternoon
6. **Subject line rule**: If the journalist can't understand the story from the subject line alone, rewrite it
