# Pre-Negotiation Checklist

Before getting on the call, define these variables.

## 1. The Numbers

- [ ] **Walk-Away Price (Floor)**: The price below which you lose money/money isn't worth it.
- [ ] **Target Price (Goal)**: The ideal number.
- [ ] **Anchor Price (Opening)**: The high number you start with (so you can land on Target).

## 2. The People

- [ ] **Who is the Decision Maker?**: Can they sign?
- [ ] **Who is the Champion?**: Who wants this to happen?
- [ ] **Who is the Blocker?**: Who loses if you win?

## 3. The Variables (Give/Get)

- [ ] **What can I give?**: (Discount, Terms, Start Date)
- [ ] **What do I want?**: (Case Study, Yearly Upfront, Intro to Parent Co)

## 4. The Exit

- [ ] **What is my BATNA?**: If this fails, do I have other deals? Do I have runway?
- [ ] **What is their BATNA?**: If they don't buy, do they fail? Do they use a spreadsheet?
