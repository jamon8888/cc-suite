# PESO Channel Reference Guide

Detailed channel specifications, benchmarks, and tactical guidance for each PESO channel. Loaded on demand when designing a campaign channel mix.

---

## PAID

### Social Advertising

| Platform | Best audience | Best format | CPM benchmark | When to use |
|---------|--------------|-------------|--------------|-------------|
| Meta (FB/IG) | B2C, broad consumer | Video (15s), Carousel, Stories | €3–12 | Mass awareness, retargeting, product launch |
| LinkedIn | B2B, professionals, execs | Sponsored content, InMail, Video | €20–60 | Thought leadership, B2B lead gen, exec audiences |
| TikTok | Under-35, entertainment-first | Short video (9–15s), TopView | €5–20 | Youth brands, cultural moments, viral plays |
| YouTube | Research-mode audiences | Skippable (15–30s), bumper (6s) | €3–10 | Consideration, demonstration, narrative storytelling |
| X/Twitter | News junkies, early adopters | Promoted posts, video | €3–8 | News hijacking, real-time moments, tech audiences |

**Budget split rule of thumb**: For brand campaigns, allocate 60% to awareness formats (video, reach), 30% to consideration (engagement, clicks), 10% to conversion (retargeting, lead gen). Reverse for performance campaigns.

### Programmatic / Display

| Format | Strength | Limitation | Best use case |
|--------|---------|-----------|---------------|
| Display banners | Reach at scale | Low attention, banner blindness | Retargeting, brand reminder |
| Native advertising | High engagement, editorial context | Higher CPM | Credibility + reach for launch |
| DOOH (Digital OOH) | Contextual targeting, high impact | Cannot click | City launches, mass awareness |
| Connected TV | Premium context, full screen | Expensive | Brand films, premium positioning |

### Influencer (Paid)

**Tier definition by follower count and strategic role:**

| Tier | Followers | CPM | Trust level | Best use |
|------|----------|-----|-------------|---------|
| Mega (celebrity) | 1M+ | High variable | Low-Medium | Mass reach, awareness |
| Macro | 100K–1M | €10–30 | Medium | Category authority |
| Micro | 10K–100K | €5–15 | High | Niche trust, authentic demo |
| Nano | 1K–10K | €2–8 | Very High | Peer recommendation, local |

**Key principle**: For brand campaigns, micro and nano influencers consistently outperform on engagement rate and purchase intent per euro spent. Mega influencers are justified only when reach at scale is the primary objective.

## EARNED

### Press / Media Relations

**Lead time by media type:**

| Media type | Lead time for coverage | Pitch format | Success metric |
|-----------|----------------------|-------------|---------------|
| National daily (Le Monde, Figaro) | 24–72 hours (news) / 2–4 weeks (features) | Short news hook or exclusivity offer | Placement + reach |
| Weekly magazine | 4–8 weeks | Story angle + visuals | Placement + audience quality |
| Trade press | 2–4 weeks | Product/sector angle | Penetration in target professional community |
| Podcast / Newsletter | 3–6 weeks | Guest angle + hook | Depth of engagement with niche audience |
| TV / Radio | News: same day / Features: 4+ weeks | Spokesperson + visual angle (TV) | Reach + authority |

**The newsworthy test (6 criteria — a story needs at least 3):**
1. **New** — Is this genuinely new information?
2. **Relevant** — Does it affect the publication's audience?
3. **Surprising** — Does it defy expectations?
4. **Human** — Is there a person / emotion at the centre?
5. **Conflict** — Is there tension, opposition, challenge?
6. **Big** — Does it involve numbers, scale, significance?

### Organic Social (Earned amplification)

Earned social requires content designed to be shared, not just consumed.

**Shareable content formula:**
- Emotionally resonant (triggers a feeling, not just a thought)
- Immediately legible (message lands in 3 seconds)
- Gives the sharer social currency (makes them look smart/caring/funny/in-the-know)
- Platform-native (format and tone match the platform's culture)

**Virality trigger types by platform:**

| Platform | Primary virality trigger | Secondary trigger |
|---------|------------------------|------------------|
| LinkedIn | Contrarian professional opinion | Success story + data |
| Instagram | Aspirational aesthetic | Behind-the-scenes authenticity |
| TikTok | Surprise / subverted expectation | Trend participation |
| X/Twitter | Strong opinion on trending topic | Clever response to news |

## SHARED

### Brand Social Channels

**Content mix benchmarks (rule of 3/3/3):**
- 1/3 educate (category knowledge, how-to, insight)
- 1/3 entertain (brand personality, culture, behind-scenes)
- 1/3 promote (product, service, campaign, proof)

If more than 1/3 of content is promotional, engagement drops significantly.

**Platform frequency benchmarks (minimum for active presence):**

| Platform | Minimum frequency | Optimal | Max before diminishing returns |
|---------|-----------------|---------|-------------------------------|
| LinkedIn | 3×/week | 5×/week | 1×/day |
| Instagram | 3×/week | 5×/week | 2×/day |
| TikTok | 3–5×/week | Daily | 3×/day |
| X/Twitter | Daily | 3–5×/day | Unlimited (if reactive) |

### Community / Forum

Relevant for B2B and niche B2C brands. Brand participation in communities (Reddit, industry Slack, Discord, LinkedIn Groups) generates high trust but requires:
- **No promotional tone** — add value before promoting
- **Consistent presence** — one-off participation is worse than absence
- **Community-specific voice** — adopt the community's register
- **Genuine expertise** — communities spot advertising instantly

## OWNED

### Website / Landing Page

**Campaign landing page conversion benchmarks:**
- Industry average: 2–5% conversion rate
- Top performers: 10–20%
- Key factors: Speed (under 3s load), single CTA, social proof above the fold

**SEO content window:** For campaigns with earned media, the spike in brand searches during press coverage must land on content that converts interest into action. Plan the owned content layer BEFORE the earned media push.

### Email / Newsletter

**Benchmark performance (French market, 2025):**

| Sector | Average open rate | Average CTR |
|--------|-----------------|------------|
| B2B / Professional services | 28–35% | 4–6% |
| Retail / E-commerce | 18–22% | 2–4% |
| Media / Publishing | 30–45% | 5–10% |
| Non-profit / Public sector | 25–35% | 3–6% |

**Campaign email timing:** Best open rates consistently on Tuesday–Thursday, 9–11 AM and 2–4 PM. Avoid Mondays (inbox overload) and Fridays (low intent).

### Events

Events are owned media's highest-attention format. Classify by objective:

| Event type | Primary objective | Metrics |
|-----------|-----------------|---------|
| Press conference / Briefing | Earned media generation | Press attendance, coverage generated |
| Product launch event | Owned narrative + earned coverage | Attendance, media hits, social amplification |
| Brand experience / Activation | Consumer engagement + UGC | Footfall, UGC generated, social reach |
| Executive roundtable | B2B relationship + thought leadership | Attendee quality, post-event coverage |
| Webinar / Virtual event | Lead gen + consideration | Registrations, completion rate, leads generated |
