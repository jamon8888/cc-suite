# /research-synthesis

Synthesize research: @$1

**Invoke the `research-synthesis` skill.**

## What to provide
- Interview transcripts or notes
- Survey results (CSV or pasted)
- Usability test notes or recordings
- Support tickets, NPS responses, app reviews

## Output includes
- Executive summary (3–4 sentences)
- Key themes with supporting quotes and prevalence counts
- Insights → Opportunities table (with impact/effort)
- User segments identified
- Prioritized recommendations (1–3)
- Open questions for further research

**Important**: Separate observations from interpretations. Quantify where possible — "7 of 10 users" is more useful than "most users."

If **~~user feedback** is connected: pull support tickets and NPS to supplement research data.
If **~~product analytics** is connected: validate qualitative findings with behavioral metrics.
If **~~knowledge base** is connected: compare against prior research and publish synthesis to repository.
